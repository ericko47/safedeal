What is SafeDeal?

SafeDeal is a secure online marketplace that connects buyers and sellers, ensuring safe transactions through an escrow system.

We provide a platform for local transactions, allowing users to buy and sell items with confidence.

How It Works

1. Agree on Product

Buyer and seller agree on price and product.

2. Buyer Sends Funds

Buyer pays via M-PESA, funds held securely in SafeDeal.

3. Seller Delivers

Item is delivered or shipped to the buyer.

4. Funds Released

Buyer confirms, SafeDeal releases funds to seller.
