What is SafeDeal?

SafeDeal is a secure online marketplace that connects buyers and sellers, ensuring safe transactions through an escrow system.

We provide a platform for local transactions, allowing users to buy and sell items with confidence.

Why SafeDeal?

Secure Payments

Escrow system holds funds until buyer confirms delivery.

Verified Users

KYC for both buyers and sellers builds trust in every transaction.

Dispute Resolution

Buyers and sellers can raise disputes and get admin support.






We are committed to providing a safe and secure platform for all your buying and selling needs.

How It Works

1. Agree on Product

Buyer and seller agree on price and product.

2. Buyer Sends Funds

Buyer pays via M-PESA, funds held securely in SafeDeal.

3. Seller Delivers

Item is delivered or shipped to the buyer.

4. Funds Released

Buyer confirms, SafeDeal releases funds to seller.
