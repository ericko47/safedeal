About SafeDeal Escrow

SafeDeal Escrow is a registered Kenyan business that exists to protect buyers and sellers during online transactions. We use a simple but powerful escrow model: funds are held safely until the item is received and approved by the buyer. This builds trust in every deal.

Whether you're selling electronics across counties or buying second-hand items from someone online, SafeDeal ensures that no one loses money unfairly. Our platform is M-PESA integrated, mobile-first, and built for trust.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and small businesses by providing a secure platform for online transactions, reducing fraud, and building economic confidence across Kenya.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital escrow platform in East Africa, trusted by millions for secure local commerce.

Our Services

  • Escrow Payments: Funds are held securely until delivery is confirmed by the buyer.
  • Verified User Accounts: Buyers and sellers verify identity using national ID to ensure trust.
  • Dispute Resolution: Our admin team steps in to mediate and resolve disputes fairly.
  • Secure M-PESA Integration: Instant mobile payments using STK Push and B2C payouts.
  • Item Posting & Browsing: Post items for sale and browse listings with unique SafeDeal IDs (SDIs).

We are committed to providing a safe and secure platform for all your buying and selling needs.







How It Works

1. Agree on Product

Buyer and seller agree on price and product.

2. Buyer Sends Funds

Buyer pays via M-PESA, funds held securely in SafeDeal.

3. Seller Delivers

Item is delivered or shipped to the buyer.

4. Funds Released

Buyer confirms, SafeDeal releases funds to seller.



Meet the Founder

SafeDeal Escrow was founded by Erick O. Ogutu, a Kenyan developer and entrepreneur based in Mombasa. Erick is passionate about solving real problems using technology. Having seen how digital scams and mistrust slow down local trade, he built SafeDeal as a practical solution for secure buying and selling.
